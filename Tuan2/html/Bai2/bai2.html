apple bavarian torte

a classic european torte baked in a springform pan cream cheese sliced almonds and apples make this the perfect

ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/3 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1(8ounce) package cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 6 apples peeled cored and sliced
  • 1/3 cup white suger
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup sliced almonds

    • directions

    1. preheat oven to 450 degrees F(230 degrees C)
    2. cream together butter,suger,vanilla and flour
    3. press crust mixture into the flat bottom of a 0 inch spring pan set aside
    4. in a medium bowl,blend cream cheese and suger beat in egg and vanilla.Pour cheese mixture over crust
    5. toss apples with suser and cinnammon.spread apple mixeture over all
    6. bake for 10 minutes reduce heat to 400 degrees f(200 degrees C) and countinue baking for 25 minutes
    7. sprinkle almonds over top of torte continue baking until lightly browned cool before remoming from pan
